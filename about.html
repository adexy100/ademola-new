My Mission

Technology should work for people — not the other way around.

I started in IT support, then cloud engineering, then software development — and somewhere along the way I realised that most business problems aren't really technical problems. They're process problems that technology can solve.

Over the past 5+ years I've worked with 40+ businesses across real estate, e-commerce, healthcare, finance, and logistics — helping them automate repetitive workflows, integrate their tools, and build IT infrastructure that scales.

My philosophy is simple: the best automation is invisible. It just works — silently saving your team hours every week while you focus on the things that truly require human attention.
